Kyoto, Japan

Why Kyoto?

Kyoto is famous for its beautiful temples, traditional tea houses, and stunning cherry blossoms. It is a cultural heart of Japan, filled with history and breathtaking scenery.

How to Get There

Kyoto can be accessed via Kansai International Airport. The Shinkansen (bullet train) from Tokyo takes around 2.5 hours.

Other Attractions

  • Kiyomizu-Dera Temple
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Forest
  • Nijo Castle
  • Philosopher's Path
