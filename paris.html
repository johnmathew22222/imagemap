Paris, France

Why Paris?

Paris, the city of love, is known for its rich history, art, and breathtaking architecture. The Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and the charming cafés make it an unforgettable destination.

How to Get There

You can reach Paris by flying into Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) or Orly Airport. The city has an excellent metro system for easy travel.

Other Attractions

  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  • Champs-Élysées
  • Seine River Cruises
