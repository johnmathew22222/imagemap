Santorini, Greece

Why Santorini?

Santorini is known for its white-washed houses, blue-domed churches, and breathtaking sunset views over the Aegean Sea. It is a paradise for relaxation and adventure.

How to Get There

Fly into Santorini National Airport or take a ferry from Athens' Piraeus Port.

Other Attractions

  • Oia Village
  • Red Beach
  • Akrotiri Archaeological Site
  • Fira Town
